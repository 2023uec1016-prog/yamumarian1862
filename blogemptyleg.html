EMPTY LEG DEALS: A NEW ERA OF PRIVATE AVIATION

Empty-leg flights are transforming private jet travel — making luxury aviation more accessible, flexible, and cost-effective than ever before.

What Are Empty Leg Flights?

Empty legs occur when a private jet flies without passengers, usually to reposition for its next scheduled flight or to return to base. These flights are offered at significantly reduced prices.

  • Jets fly empty after dropping passengers.
  • You can book them at up to 70% lower cost.
  • Same luxury aircraft — no compromise on comfort or safety.

Why Empty Leg Deals Are Gaining Popularity

Travelers are choosing empty-legs for their unmatched value and premium experience. They bring a fresh, modern advantage to private aviation.

  • Last-minute luxury travel at unbeatable pricing.
  • Enjoy the same aircraft used for VIP travel.
  • Ideal for quick business trips or spontaneous getaways.
  • Flexible travel times on select routes.

The Future of Private Jet Travel

With the rise of data-driven aviation and smarter booking technology, empty leg availability is more transparent than ever.

  • Real-time empty leg updates across regions.
  • Advanced AI routing improves jet repositioning.
  • Luxe Aviation brings global listings in one platform.
  • More travelers can now access premium private flying.
